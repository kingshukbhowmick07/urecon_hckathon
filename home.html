About AlphaBites

AlphaBites co. is a open source global news distribution website made by team Alpha Flight in Ureckathon 2023. There are primarily four sections, the first one decrypts most read articles of the day using WikiPedia API, the second one gives most read updates about certain domain (initialized with technology), third one gives most important headlines from news API, and fourth one gives what has happened on this day in history, again from WikiPedia.
